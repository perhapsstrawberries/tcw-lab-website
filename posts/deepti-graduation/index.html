Deepti Graduation

July 12, 2024

Deepti Murthy, a diligent dry lab student that specialized in single-cell sequencing analysis, has graduated from college and departs for San Diego to pursue a Ph.D. in bioinformatics. It has been a pleasure to have Deepti as a member of the lab and we all look forward to witness her exciting work in the future.
